ASTEROIDS, COMETS AND STARS

GAME SUMMARY

Asteroids, Comets and Stars requires the player to assist the space walker in his attempt to escape the ravenous hunger of the alien chasing him. To do this, you must best the alien in a series of games of Asteroids, Coments and Stars. Beat the alien in enough games and the space walker will be able to reach his space ship and escape to safety. Lose too many games, however, and the alien will eventually catch up to the space walker and devour him.

GAME INSTRUCTIONS

Asteroids, Commets and Stars operates exactly like the popular school yard game, Rock, Paper, Scissors. The only difference in this case is that instead of playing with Rocks, Paper and Scissors, we are using Asteroids, Comets and Stars. In this variation of the game, Asteroids beat Stars, Stars Beat Comets and Comets beat Asteroids. Each round is a instance of the game, with the victor of each round awarded a point. With each round won or lost by the player, the space walker will move in a given direction either towards his space ship or towards the alien. If a round ends in a tie (meaning the the user choice is the same as the computer's choice), then the space walker will remain at his current location. Once the space walker makes it to his ship(player wins) or is eaten by the alien(player loses), the gaming session is over.
